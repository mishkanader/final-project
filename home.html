What do you know about coffee?

Coffee is the one of the most addicting legal drug that can be found in a lot of things. It can be found in painkillers, green tea, and even chocolate. The average person who buys coffee outside of their home and work spend about the equivalent value of a round plane trip to Florida every year. It takes about 5 years before the coffee berries are ready to pick and one tree is the equivalent of one pound of roasted coffee!

Coffee was first discovered in Ethiopia when they noticed their goats “dancing” after eating coffee berries. At first in Africa, people ate coffee berries with fat that were like energy balls. In 1675, the King of England banned coffee because the believed that, that was were people went to conspire against him. The only state that grows coffee in the United States is Hawaii and is the second most traded commodity (right after oil) in the world. The beans (after peeled out of the red berry) are roasted for a few minutes then pop and double in size. After it pops a second time, the beans are done, kind of like popcorn.
